# Quote pack credits

In the long tradition of eggdrop/BMotion bots shipping pop-culture quote files,
some of the example packs here are short one-liners lifted from TV and film. They
are included as homage and as ready-to-use examples — **they are the property of
their respective creators and rights holders, and are *not* covered by this
project's MIT license.**

| Pack | Source | Credit |
|---|---|---|
| `classic.txt` | original | written for this project (BMotion-flavored sass) |
| `rickmorty.txt` | *Rick and Morty* | Dan Harmon & Justin Roiland / Adult Swim |
| `southpark.txt` | *South Park* | Trey Parker & Matt Stone / Comedy Central |
| `futurama.txt` | *Futurama* | Matt Groening & David X. Cohen / 20th Television |
| `squidbillies.txt` | *Squidbillies* | Dave Willis & Jim Fortier / Adult Swim |
| `timeric.txt` | *Tim and Eric Awesome Show, Great Job!* | Tim Heidecker & Eric Wareheim / Adult Swim |
| `snuffbox.txt` | *Snuff Box* | Matt Berry & Rich Fulcher / BBC |
| `orgazmo.txt` | *Orgazmo* (1997) | Trey Parker |

These are short quotations used for non-commercial, transformative/parody purposes.
If you're publishing a bot and would rather not redistribute them, delete the
packs you don't want and write your own — `classic.txt` is an original example to
start from, and adding a pack is just dropping a `.txt` file here (see the main
[README](../../README.md#adding-quotes)).
